What Are Cookies

To start they are not the kind you eat. But cookies are hidden but important part of the online experience. As long as you are using a web based browser cookies are used to help customize your interaction with the site. as well as helps save login information and personalize your experience.

Cookies are bits of data that are sent to and from your browser to link your session with the server.

Everytime you open a webpage or click on a link a piece of data is sent to the server hosting that site linking your session.

There are two primary types of cookies on the internet and those are:

  • Authentication Cookies
  • Tracking Cookies

    • Authentication cookies save a users data when they log onto a website. It could be but not limited to your username, password, which account they are using and whether they are currently logged in or not.

    Tracking cookies are cookies that "record" your page usage and help tailor your web session to your display content that you tend to visit often.
